I am Ricky Huynh, a first-year TAS and Mathematics teacher, currently undertaking a Bachelor of Education (Secondary) / Bachelor of Arts (Design Innovation and Technologies).

Teaching is a true passion of mine, as I believe the work we do for our students empowers them to become informed, capable, and contributing global citizens.

I have always enjoyed TAS as a subject throughout my own schooling, and I strive to foster and promote the creativity and innovation that this subject naturally inspires in students. Every TAS teacher has their favourite tool or machine—mine is the espresso machine!

In addition, I am trained in Hospitality (Kitchen Operations), enabling me to deliver VET Hospitality courses.

Mathematics is another subject I deeply value for its logical structure and problem-solving nature—and, of course, my favourite formula is the Sum of an Arithmetic Progression!
